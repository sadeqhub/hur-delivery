## 7. Order Process

### Creating Orders (Merchants)
1. Enter pickup address (auto-filled from store location)
2. Enter customer name, phone, and delivery address
3. Select vehicle type (bike, car, or truck)
4. Add order notes if needed
5. Confirm and submit order

### Accepting Orders (Drivers)
1. Receive order notification
2. Review order details and delivery fee
3. Accept or reject order within time limit
4. Navigate to pickup location
5. Complete pickup and delivery
6. Update status at each step

### Order Lifecycle
- **Created**: Order submitted by Merchant
- **Assigned**: Order automatically assigned to available Driver
- **Accepted**: Driver has accepted the order
- **Picked Up**: Driver has collected the item
- **On the Way**: Driver is en route to customer
- **Delivered**: Order successfully completed
- **Cancelled**: Order cancelled by Merchant
- **Rejected**: Order rejected by Driver

### Cancellations
- **Merchant Cancellation**: Can cancel before driver pickup (fees may apply)
- **Driver Rejection**: Driver can reject before pickup without penalty
- **System Cancellation**: Unassigned orders auto-cancel after timeout
- **Cancellation Fees**: May apply if order is cancelled after driver acceptance
